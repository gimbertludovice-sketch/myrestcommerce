Our Journey & Legacy

A Kitchen That Started On A Two-Burner Stove

From Condo Hustle to 600 Dishes

LUDOVICE Kitchen & Table began with two friends, a two-burner stove, and a stubborn belief that Filipino food deserves a modern stage. Today we serve 600 dishes across six categories — each one a small act of remembering home.

🍳🔥🍛

Our Timeline

How we grew from a small condominium kitchen to a live digital restaurant network.

2025
The Spark

The Two-Burner Condo Kitchen

Gimbert Ludovice and a close chef friend started cooking small-batch home-style Filipino meals out of a small condominium setup in Biñan, Laguna. Word of mouth grew fast.

2025
Expansion

First Commercial Table & Hub

Transitioned into a physical kitchen facility to handle order spikes. Expanded the menu from 15 daily specials to a full 80-dish catalog.

2025
Innovation

Fusion Meets Tradition

Introduced modern fusion categories — blending classic heritage Filipino flavors with contemporary plating and dietary labels (Healthy, Balanced, Indulgent).

2026
Digital Era

Launch of the Live Digital System

Built and launched the interactive ordering platform, complete with real-time analytics, Excel integration, and secured student authentication.

2026
600 Dishes

600 Dishes & Beyond

Reached a massive milestone of 600 curated menu items, full real-time order tracking, AI integration features, and active community monitoring.
